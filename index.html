Smk's Famous Dessert Recipe

Introduction

It is known as one of the definitive desserts of summer, this dessert recipe is easy and economical and it's called Mojito.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 500ml Milk
  • 500g Almonds
  • 200g cashew nut

Steps

  1. Reheat the oven at 250℃
  2. Reduce almond to powder
  3. Add eggs and mix until well combined.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients to the egg mixture until well combined
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the three pans and spread evenly
  6. Back for 30min
